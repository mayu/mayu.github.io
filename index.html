We reimagine electronic
instruments for live music.

Most electronic instruments are designed to be looped. As a result, an entire industry has forgotten the craft of instrumentation and improvision.

We're changing that.

We’re a team of design thinkers, music theorists, sound designers, fabric designers, hardware and software engineers,
and we're coming together to build a product that makes it easier for a modern beat maker to create live music.
